## Problem Statement

A customer says: "Our GaN RF power amplifier module is overheating. We are considering diamond, but we do not know whether we need a diamond heat spreader, diamond submount, GaN-on-Diamond, Cu-diamond composite, or conventional package improvement."

## Known Inputs

- The device is a GaN RF PA.
- The application is high-power RF.
- The present stack is likely GaN-on-SiC or a GaN device in a conventional package.
- The target is lower junction temperature and higher thermal margin.

## Unknowns

- Mechanical, RF, cost, and schedule constraints.
- Whether current overheating is die-level, package-level, or system-cooling-limited.
