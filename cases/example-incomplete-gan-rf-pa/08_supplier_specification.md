# Supplier Specification: Example Incomplete GaN RF PA

Supplier Request ID: SUP-001

## Item Or Service

Preliminary information request for candidate thermal materials or package elements, such as diamond heat spreader, diamond submount, Cu-diamond composite, or related package material.

## Required Data

- Thermal conductivity range and measurement basis.
- Compatible attach or metallization options at a public-safe level.
- Available thickness and area ranges.
- Coefficient of thermal expansion or integration notes where public-safe.
- Handling, inspection, and storage requirements.

## Required Format

Tabular supplier response with source, test method, and status for each claim.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Claims include basis and measurement method.
- Interface assumptions are stated separately from bulk material properties.
- No supplier claim is treated as validated for the customer case without review.

## Incoming Inspection

- Check dimensions and surface condition.
- Confirm material identity or supplier certificate.
- Review interface compatibility before thermal conclusions are made.

## Red Flags For Supplier Claims

- Bulk thermal conductivity presented as device-level temperature reduction.
- Missing interface or attach assumptions.
- Claims without measurement method.
- Pricing or contract language mixed into technical specification.

## Confidentiality Level

public
