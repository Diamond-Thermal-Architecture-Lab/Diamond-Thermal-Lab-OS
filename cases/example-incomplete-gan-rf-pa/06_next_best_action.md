# Next Best Action: Example Incomplete GaN RF PA

Action ID: NBA-001

## Action

Collect bounded thermal architecture inputs before selecting a diamond route or launching detailed simulation.

## Purpose

Reduce the top uncertainties: heat source geometry, interface thermal resistance, and package-to-heat-sink boundary condition.

## Owner Role

Thermal architecture lead, with input from package engineer and customer-facing technical owner.

## Priority

P1

## Required Inputs

- Bounded heat source dimensions or sanitized heat map.
- Current package stack and thermal path.
- Die attach, submount, TIM, and heat sink interface assumptions.
- Cooling boundary and temperature reference.
- RF and mechanical constraints.

## Expected Output

A narrowed architecture comparison that identifies whether package-level, spreader/submount, die-near, or system cooling routes deserve validation first.

## Decision Enabled

Whether to proceed with package improvement, diamond heat spreader/submount evaluation, die-near diamond route review, or cooling-boundary redesign.

## Estimated Effort

Low to medium, depending on customer data availability.

## Status

proposed
