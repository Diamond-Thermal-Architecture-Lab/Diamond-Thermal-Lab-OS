## Evidence Needed

- Temperature-dependent oxide and silicon property assumptions and stress-free reference condition.
- Oxidation temperature, dwell or exposure, and cooling-route record.
- Reactor, ambient, thermal-contact, and release-fixture constraint description.
- Initial and post-release profile measurement plan across membrane side lengths.
- Separate residual-stress and bow/warpage acceptance criteria.

## Guardrail

No result-derived profile or stress value is included in the blind baseline.
