# Blind Input Manifest

This repository-level benchmark freezes a blind case before evidence reveal. It is not a prospective double-blind experimental trial.

## Included Source Columns

- material: thermally grown SiO2;
- layer thickness: approximately 1.5 micrometres; and
- membrane side length: approximately 256.7, 340.5, and 506.7 micrometres.

## Excluded Until Evidence Reveal

- measured membrane profile values;
- estimated residual-stress values;
- source discussion explaining the result; and
- source title and full citation.

The listed side lengths are known geometry inputs. They are not outcome measurements and do not establish a geometry-dependent comparison basis.
