# Red Flags

case_id: literature-arxiv-2305-thermal-sio2-released-membrane
title: Released SiO2 membrane thermomechanical screening benchmark
status: draft
confidentiality_level: public

- High-temperature oxidation history is incomplete: dwell, cooling route, and reference condition require evidence.
- Reactor ambient and thermal-contact boundary definition is incomplete.
- Release-fixture mechanical constraint definition is incomplete.
- Residual-stress, initial/post-release profile, and applicable acceptance criteria are incomplete.
- Multiple membrane side lengths require a geometry-dependent comparison basis before extrapolation.
- These are evidence gaps, not predictions of an adverse outcome.
