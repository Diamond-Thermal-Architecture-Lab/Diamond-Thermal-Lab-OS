# Supplier Questions

## General Supplier Data Questions

- What exact material grade or process family is being quoted?
- What test method supports each thermal property?
- What are the units, sample geometry, and temperature conditions?
- What interface, bondline, pressure, or mounting assumptions are included?
- What incoming inspection criteria are recommended?
- What data is public-safe, customer-confidential, or restricted?

## Diamond Route Questions

- What interface thermal resistance basis is available?
- How is bonding quality inspected?
- What surface finish, metallization, or attach stack is assumed?
- What reliability evidence is available for the intended use condition?

## Claim Safety

Supplier claims should enter the claim ledger as supplier-stated basis, not as validated case results.
