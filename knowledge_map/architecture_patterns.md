# Architecture Patterns

## Package-Level Routes

- `PAT-CONV-PKG-001`: conventional package upgrade.
- `PAT-CUDIA-001`: Cu-diamond composite spreader or baseplate.
- `PAT-VAPOR-PKG-001`: vapor chamber package upgrade.

## Die-Near Or Material Routes

- `PAT-DIA-SPREADER-001`: diamond heat spreader.
- `PAT-DIA-SUBMOUNT-001`: diamond submount.
- `PAT-GAN-DIA-001`: GaN-on-Diamond or GaN bonded to diamond.
- `PAT-SIC-ALN-001`: SiC or AlN substrate/carrier upgrade.

## Cooling Architecture Routes

- `PAT-DOUBLE-COOL-001`: double-side cooling.
- `PAT-MICRO-DIA-001`: microchannel diamond hybrid.

## Use Guidance

Pattern references help structure comparison. They do not make a route validated or preferred without case evidence.
